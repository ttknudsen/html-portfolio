The Best Movies According to Tracy

My top 3 movies of all-time:

The Princess Bride

This movie is hilarious and stands the test of time. Watch the movie, then read the book, then watch the movie again! For the best viewing experience, watch the movie in a theater with a crowd reciting the lines.

Harry Potter and the Sourcerers Stone

The first of the Harry Potter series. Meet Harry as he discovers every kids daydream, that he is a wizard!

Batman

The tales of the comic book character Batman as he defends the good in the world.
